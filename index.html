About
I am a Senior Research Associate on Robert Gray's EPSRC Fellowship at the University of East Anglia. Previously, I have been a Heilbronn Research Fellow at the University of Manchester and an LMS Early Career fellow at the University of St Andrews. I completed my PhD at Heriot-Watt University in 2022, under the supervision of Laura Ciobanu. I have previously completed an MMath in Mathematics at the University of St Andrews.
My research interests are in combinatorial and geometric (semi)group theory, and the interaction between (semi)groups and formal languages.
